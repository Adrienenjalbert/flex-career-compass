# Indeed Flex - Flexible Work Platform
# Last Updated: December 2024
# Contact: support@indeedflex.com

## Company Overview
Indeed Flex is a flexible staffing platform that connects workers with temporary and flexible shifts in hospitality, warehouse, retail, and facilities management. Workers can choose when they work, get paid weekly, and access shifts through the Indeed Flex mobile app.

## Key Facts
- Platform: Mobile app (iOS and Android)
- Pay: Weekly direct deposit
- Industries: Hospitality, Warehouse/Industrial, Retail, Facilities
- Coverage: Austin, Dallas, Houston, Nashville, Atlanta, Cincinnati, Columbus, Ontario CA

## How to Get Started
1. Download the Indeed Flex app (iOS or Android)
2. Create your profile (15 minutes)
3. Complete verification (24-48 hours)
4. Browse and accept shifts
5. Get paid weekly via direct deposit

## Certifications That Increase Pay
- TABC (Texas Alcoholic Beverage Commission) - Required for bartending in Texas
- ServSafe Food Handler - Food service roles
- Forklift Certification - Warehouse roles (+$2-4/hr)
- TIPS Certification - Bartending nationwide
